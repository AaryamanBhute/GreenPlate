Welcome!

We built Green Plate to give the public the power to make a greener choice and the save our home, the Earth.

Green Plate gives consumers the tools they need to chose more eco-friendly food options to reduce their carbon footprint and take their business to restaurants that are more eco-conscious. This will incentivize businesses to invest in making greener choices in order to get business from Green Plate users who will prefer to eat at greener places. When the Green Plate community gets large enough, we will have the purchasing power to sway corporations into better ecological practices, creating a more prosperous life for us earthlings

Green Plate works by giving restaurants near you a rating based on their carbon footprints, allowing you to choose the more eco-friendly option!
