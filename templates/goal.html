Future Plans

Green Plate's future is as bright as it is green, currently our database isn't complete, however our plans to opensource the database will fix this issue. When users encounter a restaurant that is not in our eco-database, they will be given the choice to give it a rating on their own. This rating will then be verified through a dedicated team and ratings from other users

We are planning to expand Green Plate to a mobile app to make it accessible to even more people!
